## Goal Fulfillment

The `unified-agentic-framework` fulfills the user's request to transition from isolated tools to a unified agentic ecosystem.

- **Reasoning Depth**: Enhanced by cross-agent memory.
- **Safety**: Enhanced by the atomic execution mesh.
- **Efficiency**: Enhanced by automated handovers and health monitoring.
